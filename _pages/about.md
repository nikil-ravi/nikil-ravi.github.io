Hello!

I'm an MS student at Stanford specializing in AI. I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Percy Liang, and am currently working with CRFM on developing state-of-the-art foundation models while making them more accessible and open.

Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, where I worked with Prof. Julia Hockenmaier and Prof. Eliu Huerta.

I have worked on:
- Multi-modal AI and agents at Autonomi AI.
- AI for Science work, including reproducibility as well as applications in multi-messenger astrophysics, spectroscopy and more at NCSA and ANL.
- Software development focused on distributed systems and networking at Clockwork Systems, Inc.
