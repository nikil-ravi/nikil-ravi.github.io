Welcome to Nikil’s website!

I am a research scientist at Vals AI, a startup in San Francisco focused on evaluating foundation models by creating new benchmarks and evaluating newly released models in an unbiased manner.


Prior to this, I was an MS student at Stanford specializing in AI, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Percy Liang. I worked on the Marin project, an open lab for building foundation models with a state-of-the-art 8B model released and more to come! You can read more about the project here.


I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I worked with Prof. Eliu Huerta and Prof. Julia Hockenmaier.
